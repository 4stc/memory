Guide:

  1. Flip over a card by clicking on it
  2. Then flip another card
  3. If the cards match, both cards will stay face up
  4. If not, both cards will flip over and face down
  5. You win the game once all the cards are facing up
  6. The cards will then be automatically reshuffled

Optional:

  • Try playing the game with someone else and switching players each turn of the game to see who can get the most pairs
